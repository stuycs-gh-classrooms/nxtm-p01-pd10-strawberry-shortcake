### How To Play
Explain how to play your game. Include any keyboard and/or mouse commands, game objectives, etc.

You are a humble candle on your chocolate cake. Prevent the strawberries from invading and turning your chocolate world into a strawberry shortcake!

Shoot with spacebar or enter

objectives:
try to get the highest score!
dont die!
